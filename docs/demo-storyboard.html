User Journey Through the Demo

The demo follows one continuous story — a trader's journey from "overwhelmed by noise" to "informed position with TP/SL set":

1. Too much noise (problem)
2. AI distills it into narratives (Feed)
3. Narratives link to prediction markets (Predict)
4. One tap to trade the thesis on perps (Trade via Pacifica)
5. Full position management inline (TP/SL, close, portfolio)
6. Show the architecture that makes it real (tech credibility)
7. Land the brand

The story is: intelligence in, position out. pnl.fun is the bridge.
