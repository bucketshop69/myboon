# myboon Beta Discovery Plan

Status: working brief
Scope: beta discovery, Seeker launch preparation, ecosystem visibility

## Objective

Get myboon discovered before beta by showing proof of a mobile product that turns
market context into market action.

The goal is not to make a generic launch campaign. The goal is to make the right
people understand what myboon is, see that it is real, and want early access.

## Current Positioning

myboon is a context-to-action app for crypto markets.

It watches scattered signals, builds entity-first market memory, and connects the
resulting context to actions users can inspect or take from a mobile wallet-like
interface.

Short version:

```text
Fast-moving markets create scattered signals.
myboon remembers the context and connects it to action.
```

## Primary Audience

### 1. Solana Mobile / Seeker Users

These users matter first because myboon is mobile-first and should debut through
the Seeker / Solana dApp Store path.

What they care about:

- apps that feel native on mobile
- wallet-first flows
- Seeker relevance
- useful market actions, not dashboards copied from desktop

Message:

```text
myboon is built for the mobile market user: context, markets, and positions in
one wallet-like app.
```

### 2. Solana Ecosystem Builders

These people can give feedback, intros, credibility, and distribution.

What they care about:

- real shipping progress
- good Solana-native UX
- clear use of mobile distribution
- a product that brings new consumer behavior to Solana

Message:

```text
myboon is testing a Solana-native mobile loop: understand what moved, open the
relevant market, and track what happens next.
```

### 3. Crypto Market Users

This includes Polymarket users, perps traders, and people who follow wallets,
funding rates, prediction markets, and macro narratives.

What they care about:

- context before action
- knowing why a market moved
- not starting from zero every time
- fewer scattered tabs and tools

Message:

```text
myboon turns market noise into remembered context around entities like markets,
wallets, protocols, events, and narratives.
```

### 4. Grant / Ecosystem Reviewers

These people should see myboon as a serious mobile product with a clear market
and a credible path to users.

What they care about:

- why this should exist in the ecosystem
- what has already been built
- why mobile matters
- whether there is a clear beta and distribution plan

Message:

```text
myboon brings market intelligence and action into a mobile Solana experience,
starting with prediction markets, perps signals, and entity memory.
```

## Content Pillars

### 1. Build Logs

Simple weekly updates showing what shipped.

Examples:

- entity memory pipeline
- Hyperliquid perps connector
- Polymarket market signals
- Penpot design organization
- wallet-like mobile UX improvements

### 2. Market Context Examples

Use real examples to show the problem.

Examples:

- a Polymarket odds jump
- funding-rate movement
- a wallet making a large bet
- a geopolitical headline moving oil or prediction markets

The point is not the news itself. The point is:

```text
One headline is not enough. Users need the entity timeline behind it.
```

### 3. Product Demos

Show the app, even if the flow is rough.

Examples:

- open the feed
- tap a market
- see the context
- inspect the action
- track a position

### 4. Design / UX

Show that myboon is not trying to become another complicated trading terminal.

Examples:

- wallet-like home
- feed to market flow
- market detail screen
- positions screen
- Penpot system organization

### 5. Beta Requests

Ask for testers directly.

Examples:

- "I am looking for 10 Seeker users to test this."
- "I want feedback from people who trade prediction markets or perps."
- "If you follow market narratives and hate scattered context, I want to talk."

## This Week Sprint

### Day 1: Make The Beta Surface

Prepare one place to send people.

Minimum:

- one-line positioning
- short product explanation
- 3 to 5 screenshots or demo GIF
- early access form
- clear target user

Beta page copy:

```text
myboon is a mobile context-to-action app for crypto markets.

It remembers market context around entities like markets, wallets, protocols,
events, and narratives, then connects that context to actions you can inspect
from one mobile app.
```

### Day 2: Publish The Current Build Log

Post the weekly update from the personal account first. Repost from myboon.

Angle:

```text
This week was about organizing context.
```

Talk about:

- entity memory pipeline
- Hyperliquid perps connector started
- Penpot used to organize product screens
- why this helps the app move toward beta

### Day 3: Do Manual Outreach

Send short DMs to 20 to 30 relevant people.

Target:

- Solana Mobile builders
- Superteam India members
- people posting about Seeker
- Polymarket / perps users
- friendly crypto builders

DM structure:

```text
Hey, I am building myboon, a mobile context-to-action app for crypto markets.
I am preparing the Seeker beta path and looking for early feedback from people
who care about mobile crypto UX or market context.

Would you be open to trying an early build when it is ready?
```

### Day 4: Prepare Seeker / dApp Store Assets

Create a draft package before final submission.

Minimum assets:

- app name
- short description
- long description
- screenshots
- app icon
- privacy policy URL
- support URL
- beta/demo video
- mobile wallet flow explanation

### Day 5: Prepare Grant One-Pager

Make one reusable grant narrative.

Sections:

- problem
- solution
- why Solana Mobile / Seeker
- what is already built
- what the grant unlocks
- next 30 days
- links to demo, screenshots, repo, website

### Day 6: Publish One Demo Clip

Do not explain everything. Show one flow.

Best demo:

```text
Signal appears -> myboon explains context -> user opens related market -> user
can track what happens next.
```

### Day 7: Review Signals

Track:

- replies
- DMs
- waitlist signups
- useful intros
- people asking to test
- confusing comments
- which sentence made people understand the product

## What Not To Lead With

Do not lead with:

- "AI knowledge graph"
- "entity manager"
- "pipeline architecture"
- "multi-source source-agnostic feed"

These are useful builder details, but they are not the first user-facing hook.

Lead with:

```text
Markets move fast. Context gets scattered. myboon remembers what matters and
connects it to action.
```

## First Launch Milestone

The first serious milestone should be:

```text
myboon beta prepared for Seeker / Solana dApp Store distribution.
```

This gives the content, grants, and outreach a concrete center.

Everything should point back to one proof:

```text
There is a real mobile app, built for Seeker-style crypto users, that helps them
understand fast-moving markets and act from one place.
```
