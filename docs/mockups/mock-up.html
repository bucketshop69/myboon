Navigation

Feed, Markets, Positions, Wallet maps to the user's journey. Markets becomes the app hub: predictions, perps, swaps, DEXs, funding, and future integrations.

Feed Cards

Cards lead with the move, then the evidence, then the action. The layout avoids article behavior and makes the feed feel like a live desk.

Positions

Each position keeps its originating signal. That makes myboon accountable: the user can see whether feed intelligence turned into better trades.

Visual Tone

The palette uses #073B4C as the base, #FFD166 and #118AB2 as text/accent colors, and #06D6A0 / #EF476F strictly for financial up/down movement.
